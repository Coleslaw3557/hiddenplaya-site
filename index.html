The Maze

Test your might! Race through the maze dodging cops, rope, and other obstacles, or just explore the rooms!

Camp

We're mostly a bunch of 30s/40s, from California, Austin, Portland, and Salt Lake. We cap the camp at approx 35 people so everyone still knows each other.

Camp owned infrastructure:

  • Couches, a stove, cooler storage, and somewhere to sit
  • A private shower
  • A covered, lit, shade structure for tent campers
  • 500 gallons of water (no, there's no pool)
  • Some 110v power — enough to charge a phone, not a hair dryer, or aircon.

What to bring yourself:

  • Your own food
  • Your own sleep setup — optionally shade, you bring the rest
  • RVs: we're not speced to have RVs in camp so we're probably not a good fit if you need that
